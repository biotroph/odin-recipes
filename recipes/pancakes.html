Pancakes Recipe


Supposedly easier than you think

Ingredients

  • pancake mix
  • oil
  • milk
  • egg

Steps

  1. Oil skillet over medium-high heat (when drop of water sprinkle disappear)
  2. Stir all ingredients until blended
  3. Pour 1/4 cup onto skillet and cook until edges are dry (1 min 30 seconds)
  4. Turn and cook until golden or 1 minute 30 seconds.
