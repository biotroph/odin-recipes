French Toast Recipe

Made with eggs, milk and bread. And place on the stove.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • egg
  • milk
  • spice
  • salt
  • butter

Steps

  1. Whisk milk, egg, spice, salt together
  2. Butter skillet over medium-high heat
  3. Dunk bread in egg mixture on both side
  4. Place on skillet for 4 minutes each side until golden
  5. Serve hot
